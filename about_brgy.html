ABOUT THE BARANGAY

Barangay 599 Zone 59 is a barangay in District VI of Manila, located in Peralta Street, Old Sta Mesa. Consists of 10 puroks, it covers about 4.5 hectares. As of 2014, the number of residents is approximately 5,600.
