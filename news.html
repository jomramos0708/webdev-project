FEBRUARY 01, 2016

Barangay Fiesta 2016


This year's Barangay Fiesta will be held on February 10-11, 2016. The pogram will officially starts at 8:00 AM in the Barangay Court. Expect this year's fiesta to be more fun and exciting! Battle of the bands, Binibining Barangay 2016, Barangay Hataw will be happened on the second day. Please wait for further announcements! See you there!

DECEMBER 16, 2015

Barangay 599 held its "Annual Gift-Giving" 2015


Barangay 599 Zone 59 Officials, with the leadership of Hon. Salvador C. Onanad, held the annual gift-giving for this year of 2015. 150 children of this barangay received school supplies. Next year's target is to reach out 300 children. Our barangay officials are hopeful to give more gifts not only for children, but also for the whole barangay.

DECEMBER 02, 2015

PUP IT students to create a Barangay Information System


Last December 1, 3rd year IT students from Polytechnic University of the Philippines came to interview Chairman Onanad about their proposal of Barangay Information System. An information system will help the barangay to automate and produce reliable reports faster and easier. The said project will be developed for almost a year however, our barangay officials are thankful for this kind of project.

NOVEMBER 10, 2015

Say no to malnutrition: Barangay 599 rice distribution


Barangay officials intiated rice giving to the residents, with the help of City of Manila. Each family received 5 kilos of rice, which can be a big help to lessen the residents' budget. More than 300 families received rice from the barangay.

JULY 08, 2015

New equipments for better public service


For this year barangay budget, the officials brought new equipments in the barangay that will be helpful in differenct services. Some of these are printers, computer cabinet, table, computer peripherals and a motorcycle for barangay services.

MARCH 28, 2015

Barangay General Assembly: Serbisyong Tapat at Sapat para sa lahat


The traditional and annual barangay assembly was held last March 28, 2015. Chairman Buddy Onanad initiated the program with the help of his barangay officials. This year's goal is to proveide better services for the residents of Barangay 599 Zone 59.
